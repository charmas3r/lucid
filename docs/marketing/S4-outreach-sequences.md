# S4 — Outreach Sequences  ·  FROZEN

> Final verdict: **weighted 89.7 · passed ✅** (3 iterations). See `pipeline-log.md` §S4.

Value-first sequences: every first touch leads with a free, specific diagnosis before any
ask. Two wedges, two audit scripts with correctly different checkpoints. One cadence with
an explicit stop rule. Throughput target: **15 video audits/week** (from S3).

---

## The first touch is always a recorded video audit

Not a cold email with a pitch. A **3–5 minute Loom-style screen recording** of *their*
actual business on Evan's screen, sent with a short personal note. It is undeniable proof
of effort, demonstrates the exact skill being sold, and earns the reply. Free, scalable
solo, no ad spend (U4/U5).

**Recording standard (both wedges):** show their real site/GBP on screen, talk like a
person, name the city and business, end with *one* low-friction next step. No slides.

---

## W1 video-audit script — established trades (~4 min)

> Target: trade ranking 4–15, weak site/reviews, maybe buying shared leads.

1. **Open (15s, personal):** "Hey [Name], Evan here — I run a small web studio in
   Escondido. I was looking at [trade]s in [City] and pulled up [Business]. Made you a quick
   2-minute teardown, no pitch — just thought you'd want to see it."
2. **Rankings checkpoint:** screen-share the Google search "[trade] [city]". Show where they
   land vs. the map-pack top 3. "Right now [competitor] is getting this call, not you."
3. **GBP checkpoint:** their Google Business Profile — categories, photos, review count vs.
   the top 3, posting recency. "You've got [N] reviews, they've got [M] — that gap is the #1
   ranking lever and it's free to close."
4. **Website/speed checkpoint:** load their site on mobile, narrate the slowness / missing
   "[city] [trade]" page / no clear phone CTA. "On a phone this takes [X]s — most callers
   bounce before it loads."
5. **Lead-broker checkpoint (W1-specific):** "If you're paying Angi/Thumbtack for shared
   leads — those go to 4 contractors at once. Ranking here means the call is *yours*."
6. **Close (the ask):** "If it's useful, I'll send the full written audit free and we can
   talk about getting you ranking. Reply here or call/text me at 858-215-4894."

**Ask terminates in (S2):** free written audit → **SEO Foundation Fix → Grow** (lead with
the 30% bundle).

## W2 video-audit script — new service businesses (~3 min)

> Target: 0–18 month business, Linktree/IG/FB-only or no site. **Different checkpoints** —
> they have no rankings to critique; the issue is *legitimacy and being found at all*.

1. **Open (15s, warm):** "Hi [Name], congrats on [Business] — saw you just opened in
   [City]. I'm Evan, I build sites for new local businesses out of Escondido. Made you a
   quick look at how you show up online right now."
2. **Legitimacy checkpoint (W2-specific):** Google their name. "Here's what a customer sees
   when they look you up — right now it's an Instagram and a Linktree. That makes people
   wonder if you're real/permanent."
3. **Findability checkpoint:** "There's no website and [no / unclaimed] Google Business
   Profile, so you're invisible for '[service] [city]' searches — that's the search your
   ideal customer is typing right now."
4. **Trust/conversion checkpoint:** "Even your IG sends people to a link tree, not a place
   to book or contact you. Every tap there is a customer you can lose."
5. **The on-ramp (W2-specific):** "I actually offer a free starter site to get you a real
   presence fast — and a flat $900 launch when you're ready for the real thing, live in
   about two weeks, built to show up on Google."
6. **Close (the ask):** "Want me to spin up the free starter so you have something real to
   point people to? Reply or text 858-215-4894."

**Ask terminates in (S2):** `/free` starter → **Launch $900 / Launch Pro $1,800** → Care Plan.

## Email + follow-up cadence (both wedges)

Same skeleton, wedge-appropriate copy. **Touch count: 5. Explicit stop after touch 5.**

| Touch | Day | Channel | Content | Ask |
|------:|----:|---------|---------|-----|
| 1 | 0 | Email (or IG DM for W2) | Personal note + **video audit link** | Soft: "worth a reply?" |
| 2 | +3 | Email reply | "Did the video come through? Here's the one thing I'd fix first: [specific]." | Re-offer next step |
| 3 | +7 | Phone **call + text** | Reference the audit; offer the free written audit / free starter | Book 15-min call |
| 4 | +12 | Email | Drop a **proof asset** (Recovery Labz / Coin-Plugz result, or a NC client win) | "Want the same?" |
| 5 | +18 | Email **break-up** | "I'll stop here — leaving the audit with you. If [season/opening] gets busy, you know where I am." | Door left open |

**Stop rule (deterministic):** stop immediately on any reply, call booked, or explicit no.
Otherwise stop after touch 5. Re-enter the sequence only after **90 days** or a trigger
event (new season for W1, grand-opening for W2). No more than 5 touches per cycle — ever.

**Value-first guard:** touches 1–2 give before they ask. No pricing in touch 1. No "just
checking in" empty pings — every touch carries a *specific* observation or asset.
