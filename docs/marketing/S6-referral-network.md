## 6a · Partner archetypes — touch the same clients, don't compete

Each partner serves Evan's exact ICP at a different moment and would *never* build a site
themselves — so referrals flow both ways without rivalry.

| Archetype | Why they fit | They meet the client when… | Reciprocal value to them |
|-----------|--------------|----------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Graphic / brand designers** (freelance, NC) | Make logos/brand but don't build/host or do SEO | client just rebranded, needs a site | Evan sends every "I need a logo first" lead |
| **Commercial photographers / videographers** (Carlsbad, Encinitas) | Shoot the imagery a site needs | new business needs photos for launch | Evan's launch clients all need a shoot |
| **Print shops / sign makers** (Escondido, San Marcos, Vista) | Serve every new local biz (cards, vehicle wraps, signage) | a business is *starting* — exactly W2 timing | Evan refers wrap/sign work; co-promote |
| **Bookkeepers / CPAs & business attorneys** | Onboard brand-new LLCs (W2) and growing trades (W1) | client forms the entity / files DBA | trusted-advisor cross-referral |
| **Marketing/ad freelancers** (PPC, social) | Run ads but need a fast, converting site + CRO under them | client's ads convert poorly | Evan does the site/CRO, they keep the ad retainer |
| **Trade suppliers & general contractors** | GCs hire subs (the W1 trades) and know who's growing | a sub needs more work/leads | Evan helps their subs rank; GC looks good |

**Disqualify as partners:** full-service agencies and other web devs (direct competitors).

## 6b · Incentive structure — concrete referral terms

Simple, honest, solo-manageable. Pick per relationship; default is the **flat finder's fee**.

| Mechanism | Terms | Use with |
|-----------|-------|----------|
| **Flat finder's fee** | **$150** per closed W2 Launch · **$250** per closed W1 Foundation Fix/retainer | designers, photographers, print shops, CPAs |
| **Recurring share** | **10% of the first 6 months** of any retainer/care plan they source | marketing freelancers, GCs sending repeat work |
| **Reciprocal (no cash)** | Mutual referral + a free Lucid landing page / audit for *their* business | partners who'd rather trade than take cash |
| **Client-side referral** | Existing client who refers another paying client → **1 free month** of their care plan/retainer (or $150 credit) | every happy client (tie to S5 review ask) |

**Rules that keep it clean:** paid only on a *closed, paid* deal; one-time fees paid within
30 days of first client payment; recurring share stops if the client churns; everything
tracked in the same CRM as S3/S7. A one-page partner sheet (terms + the S5 proof links)
is the only collateral — free to make.

## 6c · Presence plan — become "the web person" in named rooms

Show up consistently in a *small* number of real NC rooms rather than spraying everywhere.
Solo-sustainable cadence.

| Room (named) | Cadence | What Evan does |
|--------------|---------|----------------|
| **Escondido Chamber of Commerce** (home base) | join + 1 event/mo | member; offer a free "get-found-on-Google" mini-audit at mixers |
| **San Marcos / Vista / Carlsbad chambers** | 1 event/mo rotating | networking; collect new-member intel (feeds S3 W2) |
| **BNI or LeTip chapter (1 NC chapter)** | weekly, take the "web/marketing" seat | locks the category — the referral engine's core |
| **Nextdoor** (Escondido/San Marcos/Vista neighborhoods) | 2–3 helpful posts/mo | answer "who builds websites?" / "why am I not on Google?" — never spammy |
| **Local trade & contractor meetups / supply-house counters** | monthly | be the web guy who *gets* contractors (W1) |
| **Facebook groups** (NC small-biz, "Shop Escondido," city biz groups) | weekly value post | free tips + occasional case study (S5) |
| **1 free workshop / quarter** | quarterly | "Get Your Business Found on Google" at a chamber/library/coworking space — pure value, collects W1+W2 leads |

**The positioning goal:** when anyone in these rooms hears "I need a website" or "I'm not
showing up on Google," the reflexive answer is **"Talk to Evan at Lucid."** The S1 referral
one-liner is the script partners and members repeat.

**This-week starter (solo, free):** (1) join Escondido Chamber, (2) visit one BNI/LeTip
chapter as a guest, (3) draft the one-page partner sheet, (4) line up 3 designers/print
shops for a coffee with the reciprocal offer.
