## Wedge W1 — Local SEO for home-service & trade businesses

**Buyer's words:** *"I'm busy in spring, dead in winter, and I'm tired of paying HomeAdvisor/Angi for shared leads."*

### Positioning statement
> For established North County home-service contractors who are invisible on Google
> outside of paid lead apps, Lucid turns your website and Google Business Profile into a
> lead engine that ranks for "[trade] near me" in your own city — so the call goes to you,
> not the lead broker. Unlike a generic agency, you work directly with one senior engineer
> who shows you the audit, the numbers, and the plan before you pay a dollar.

### ICP (qualify a list against this)
| Dimension | Spec |
|-----------|------|
| **Verticals (named)** | HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, pool service/repair, hardscape & landscape, garage doors, pest control, painting, tree service |
| **Geography** | Escondido, San Marcos, Vista, Oceanside, Carlsbad, Encinitas (primary); Poway, Rancho Bernardo, Temecula (secondary) |
| **Business stage** | Established 2+ years, has revenue, ≥1 truck/crew, owner-operator or small team |
| **Qualifying signals** | (a) ranks below the map pack for "[trade] [city]"; (b) website is a slow Wix/GoDaddy/Facebook-only presence OR no site; (c) has <40 Google reviews OR rating <4.6; (d) currently buys shared leads (Angi/HomeAdvisor/Thumbtack/Yelp ads) |
| **Disqualify** | National franchises with corporate marketing; anyone already ranking #1–3 organically with 100+ reviews; pure new-construction subcontractors with no consumer search demand |

### Why Lucid over a generic dev/agency
1. **You see the diagnosis first** — a recorded screen audit of your actual rankings and
   site, free, before any pitch (ties to S4).
2. **One senior operator, not an account manager** — the person auditing is the person building.
3. **Numbers, not vibes** — 100/100 Lighthouse, measurable load times, tracked conversions
   (CRO analytics ship in the Foundation Fix).
4. **Local-by-proof** — Lucid's own site ranks for "[city] web design / SEO" across 10 NC
   cities; we eat our own cooking.

## Wedge W2 — Launch packages for new service-based businesses

**Buyer's words:** *"I'm opening/just opened and I need a real website — not a $3k agency quote and not a weekend I don't have."*

### Positioning statement
> For brand-new North County service businesses that need to look legitimate on day one,
> Lucid gets you a fast, found-on-Google website and a claimed Google Business Profile in
> about two weeks — flat-priced, no agency runaround. You launch with the technical
> foundation (speed, structured data, tracking) most businesses don't add until they can
> afford to redo everything.

### ICP (qualify a list against this)
| Dimension | Spec |
|-----------|------|
| **Verticals (named)** | New med-spas/wellness & aesthetics, therapists/counseling practices, personal trainers & boutique fitness, home-service startups (cleaning, mobile detailing, handyman), salons/barbers, real-estate agents, consultants/coaches, mobile pet groomers, photographers |
| **Geography** | Same NC footprint; weighted toward Encinitas/Carlsbad (wellness, boutique) and Escondido/San Marcos/Vista (home-service startups) |
| **Business stage** | 0–18 months old; has an LLC/DBA but no real website, or a single Linktree/Instagram/Facebook page |
| **Qualifying signals** | (a) new business filing or new GBP with no website link; (b) active Instagram but no site; (c) "coming soon" / parked domain; (d) recently signed a commercial lease |
| **Disqualify** | Pre-revenue idea stage with no funding or commitment; product/ecommerce-first brands (different motion); anyone needing custom app work (back-pocket capability, not the wedge) |

### Why Lucid over Squarespace-DIY or a $3k+ agency
1. **Done-for-you in ~2 weeks, flat-priced** — no DIY weekend, no open-ended agency invoice.
2. **Built to be *found*, not just exist** — local SEO foundation + GBP setup baked in.
3. **A real runway** — the launch site upgrades cleanly into the SEO care plans (S2) as
   they grow; no rebuild later.
4. **The `/free` on-ramp** — Evan can literally start them with a free starter site, then
   earn the upgrade.
